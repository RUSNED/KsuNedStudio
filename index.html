Обо мне

Ксюша ебашит лучше всех My name is Hayley and I am a Registered Physiotherapist with a special focus in Pelvic Health and Perinatal Care. I began my studies at the University of Toronto in 2012, majoring in Human Biology and Physiology. After my undergraduate, I stayed at the University of Toronto to complete my Master of Science in Physical Therapy. My journey in Pelvic Health began in the first year of my Masters — I had a strong passion for women’s health and joined the Women’s Health Division (WHD) of the Canadian Physiotherapy Association (CPA). It was during my time there that I was introduced to the field of Pelvic Health. I went on to complete specialized internships in Pelvic Health in both private practice as well as the TAPMI and Breast Cancer clinics at Women’s College Hospital.
